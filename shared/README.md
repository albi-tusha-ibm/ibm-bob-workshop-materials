## 💡 Tips for Success

### Effective Prompting
- **Be specific**: "Analyze the authentication flow in user-service.ts" vs "Explain this code"
- **Provide context**: Tell Bob what you're trying to accomplish
- **Ask follow-ups**: Refine Bob's responses with additional questions
- **Reference files**: Direct Bob to specific files when needed

### Learning Strategy
- **Start simple**: Begin with basic queries and build complexity
- **Experiment**: Try different phrasings to see what works best
- **Learn from examples**: Study the sample prompts to understand patterns
- **Practice regularly**: Use Bob daily to build proficiency

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- ❌ Vague prompts: "Fix this" or "Make it better"
- ❌ Assuming Bob knows your context without providing it
- ❌ Not verifying Bob's suggestions before implementing
- ❌ Giving up after one attempt - rephrase and try again
